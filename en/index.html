    Hermes Music Mexico

    Hermes Music is the largest distribution and retail company for the spanish speaking entertainment industry. We represent the greatest brands worldwide and have been operating in the United States since 1982 and Mexico since 1988. We also have 2 different types of stores for the music industry in Texas and 56 stores throughout México.

    About Us

    Hermes Music is the largest distribution company in the Spanish speaking entertainment industry

    Hermes Music Professional

    Broadcast and Recording

    Mainly focused on radio stations, television networks, telecommunications companies, recording and post production studios; providing all necessary support free of charge, in order to reach the maximum operating level of every product obtained.

    Live

    Backline

    In charge of professional musical instruments such as drums, percussions, guitars, bass, keyboards, synths and audio equipment required by our clients, including every accessory.

    Install

    The install department is dedicated to all special installation projects that include professional audio, lighting and LED screens. We offer permanent installation equipment for convention centers, movie theaters, standard theaters, stores, churches, schools, gyms, conference rooms, airports and every kind of establishment that may require our product and / or services.

    Lighting

    This division offers professional lighting equipment and support during the first performances with the products obtained, in order to reach their maximum level.

    Video

    Department Stores

    Hermes Music is the only audio equipment and musical instruments company in Mexico that has managed to have exclusive spaces in department stores, reaching millions of customers.

    Department Stores

    Department stores have been our clients since 1995. We started the musical instruments, professional and digital audio market in departments stores, giving direct access to our products for customers all over Mexico.

    M. I. Distribution

    Since all musical instrument’s stores located in Mexico happen to be our client, our M.I. distribution department has a coverage that includes every part of national territory.

    M.I. Distribution

    One of the keys to success in the market for Hermes Music in Mexico, has been trying to understand every single difficulty that the Mexican music industry faces in order to access musical instruments and high quality equipment at a convenient price.

    In 1988 after accessing the market in Mexico, Hermes Music expanded its operations and took musical instruments to a nationwide distribution, opening several service centers throughout the country.

    Regional sales executives travel all over the country, while our sales team, telemarketers and product experts support all of our clients in over 370 stores.

    Service Department
