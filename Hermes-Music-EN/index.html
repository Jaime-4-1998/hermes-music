    Hermes Music

    BECAUSE WE LOVE MUSIC

    It all started in 1982 with the vision of a musician. He had the dream of giving the musicians what he would’ve liked to have had when he went in search of an instrument to make a living; excellent service, fair prices, and most importantly... respect. It was with that simple formula that a small music store in McAllen, TX became one of the largest and most respected distribution companies for audio, lighting, and musical instruments in the world. Yes, Hermes Music has achieved something that few companies have done; start from the bottom and reach a prime place in the music industry at the top. This is an industry that is constantly evolving with new products and emerging technologies. Hermes Music is always ahead and always thinking of those who really matter... musicians, engineers, producers and more importantly... the dreamers.
